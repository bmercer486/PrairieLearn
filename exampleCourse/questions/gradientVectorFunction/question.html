$$ {\bf f(x)} = \begin{pmatrix} {{params.a1}} x_1^2 + {{params.a2}} x_2 \\ {{params.b1}} x_2^3 - {{params.b2}} x_1^2 \\ -{{params.c1}} x_1^2 + {{params.c2}} x_2 \end{pmatrix} $$

Obtain the gradient of ${\bf f(x)}$ at ${\bf x_o} = \left[ x_1 , x_2 \right] = \left[ {{params.x1}} , {{params.x2}} \right]$
